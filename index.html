Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How do I register for an event? →
Browse events, click Register, fill in your details, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately with your registration ID.
Can I register for multiple events? →
Yes. There is no limit on how many events you can register for, as long as there are no scheduling conflicts.
Will I receive a certificate? →
Participation certificates are issued for most events. Competition winners receive separate awards. Certificates are sent to your registered email after the event.
Is there a registration fee? →
Most events are free for KIT students. Some external competitions or premium workshops may have a nominal fee, which is clearly mentioned on the event page.
How will I get event updates? →
All updates — schedule changes, venue updates, results — are sent to your registered email. You can also check the platform's Notification Centre.
Can students from other colleges participate? →
Selected events like the Tech Symposium and Hackathon are open to students from other institutions. Event pages clearly indicate eligibility criteria.
